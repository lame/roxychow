Our Mission Statement

We want to make the best quality food we can for the dogs that we love. This means our food is fresh, non-processed, and made with the highest quality ingredients available. Through simple ingrediants and simple cooking we create a wholesome and nutritious meal high in fiber and protein with a low glycemic index that your dog is sure to love.

Our Product

Simple is the name of the game, Roxy Chow is made from fresh chicken, wholesome barley, and nutritious broccoli, that's it! Roxy Chow is high in fiber so it's sure to keep your dog satiated all day, non-processed for a back-to-nature taste, and is specially formulated with a low glycemic index to keep diabetic pups happy and healthy.
